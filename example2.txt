thermostat rulebase

If temperature is high or  current is high then change_in_current is decrease
Rule2: If temperature is medium then change_in_current is no_change
Rule3: If temperature is low and current is high then change_in_current is no_change
Rule4: If temperature is low and current is low then change_in_current is increase

temperature

decrease -50 -50 50 50
no_change 0 0 50 50
